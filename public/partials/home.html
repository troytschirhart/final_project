This app can be used to find the total value of a portfolio of stocks. Once a user logs in, they can enter the stock symbols, stock names, number of shares of each stock, and the total purchase price for each stock. The app then pulls the latest prices for each stock entered and calculates the current value of the holding along with the amount of profit (or loss). The app sums the total value of the portfolio and the total profit (or loss).
